Replication of:
Rural Roads and Local Economic Development

Term paper for the Microeconometrics course
(2on year, 1st term 2022)

You can check the complete paper HERE.

The objective was to replicate the main results of the paper and extend the analysis focusing in the methods. The paper is the following:

Asher, Sam and Paul Novosad (Mar. 2020). “Rural Roads and Local Economic Development”. American Economic Review 110.3, pp. 797–823.

Introduction

In this section I will replicate the main tables and plots from the paper and I will comment on them. The replication will not focus much on the economic theory or data preprocessing, but on evaluating carefully the empirical design. I will analyze the underlying assumptions in detail and I will test them, providing the necessary robustness checks. As an extension of the paper, I will develop some methodological points that I think are not discussed enough in the paper.
