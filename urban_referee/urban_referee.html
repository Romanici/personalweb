Referee report for:
Delivery in the city: Differentiated products competition among New York restaurants
(Sam and Novosad 2020)

Urban Economics (2on year, 2on term 2022)

The assignment consisted in writting a fake referee report for the following chosen paper:

Nathan Schiff, Jacob Cosman, Tianran Dai, Delivery in the city: Differentiated products competition among New York restaurants, Journal of Urban Economics, Volume 134, 2023, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jue.2022.103509.

It is a very interesting paper because it studies the response to entry in the restaurant sector in New York city (a large market with differentiated products), using a novel dataset and empirical strategy.
They find that restaurants frequently adjust prices and product offerings but they do not find evidence that restaurants respond differentially to new competition.

Just as a note: In the restaurant market, restaurants compete not only in prices but also in the quality of their food or space and in advertising, and thus, we say it is a market with a monopolistic competition structure.
